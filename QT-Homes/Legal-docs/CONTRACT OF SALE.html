  4. COVENANTS AND WARRANTIES
    1. Seller's Covenants

      2. a. The Seller shall sell the Property free from encumbrances other than any matter which the Seller does not and could not reasonably know about;

      b. The Seller covenants with the Buyer that the Property is completely free from all mortgages, charges and encumbrances or adverse third party rights.

      3. a. The Buyer shall pay the Purchase Price to the Seller in accordance with clause 2 above.

      c. The Buyer shall perform all its obligations in accordance with the terms of this Contract of Sale

  5. INDEMNITY
    6. The Seller affirms that it shall fully indemnify the Buyer against all losses, damages, claims, costs, fees, penalties, expenses and demands, which the Buyer may suffer in consequence of:

    a. A defect in the title to the [Demised] Property;

    b. any outstanding utility bill, service charges, and all lawful taxes, rates, assessments, dues and duties, charged on the Property to the Government or Municipality or other Local Authority which the Seller failed to pay;

    c. any action taken by any third party asserting a claim or adverse title, interest or right to the [Demised] Property; or

    d. any other encumbrance on the [Demised] Property.

  6. ENTIRE UNDERSTANDING
    1. This Contract of Sale contains the entire understanding between the parties hereto concerning the assignment and dealings of the [Demised] Property. There are no representations, agreements, arrangements or understandings oral or written between the parties hereto relating to the subject matter of this Contract of Sale which are not expressed herein.
    2. This Contract of Sale cannot be modified, changed or extended except in writing and signed by both parties.

  7. CONFIDENTIALITY
    1. The parties agree that all information received in the course of this transaction shall be treated with confidentiality.
    2. The parties agree to:
      a. Hold all confidential, non-public information received from each other or their affiliates or their respective representatives strictly confidential.

      b. Use or release or permit the use or release of such information only in the performance of this Contract of Sale and not use or release any of such information for any other purposes.

      c. If there is any conflict between information received from any competent authority relating to any matter and any statement made by or on behalf of the Seller in respect of the same matter, the Buyer shall rely on the information received from the competent authority to the exclusion of that given by or on behalf of the Seller.


  8. ASSIGNMENT
    9. No party shall assign or purport to assign or transfer any of its rights or obligations herein without the prior written consent of the other party

  9. COUNTERPARTS
    10. This Agreement may be executed in any number of counterparts and the counterparts taken together shall be deemed to constitute one and the same agreement.

  10. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION
    1. This agreement and any dispute or claim arising out of or in connection with it or its subject matter or formation (including non-contractual disputes or claims) shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.
    2. The Parties hereto shall initially attempt to resolve all claims, disputes or controversies arising under, out of or in connection with this Agreement by conducting negotiations amongst themselves. Where the Parties hereto are unable to resolve the matter following negotiations in good faith, the matter shall thereafter be resolved by recourse to litigation.
