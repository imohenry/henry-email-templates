Introducing, The All New Quickteller Service VAS Portal

Back to making the most of opportunities, here is introducing the all new Quickteller Service VAS Portal - which allows you manage your VAS profile with real- time analytic tools for business insights.

With features which include Intuitive and Analytic Dashboard, User Management, Transaction Reporting, Wallet Management and Epins Management, this portal will help you better manage and keep track of your business at a glance.

The VAS Portal features include:

Dashboard: Provides transaction and wallet/virtual card balance information, allowing merchants to track transaction volumes, success rates, and pending transactions by type and duration.

Transaction Reports Flexible reports that can be filtered by transaction type, date, biller, and channels.

Settlement Reports Contains details on total commission earned, number of transactions, and total amount, with filters for transaction type and date.

Payment Instrument Real-time view of card or wallet balances, with options to filter by date or request reference.

User Management: Merchants can easily view and manage portal users, including adding, editing, or removing users. User creation is simple, requiring the selection of user role and entering the email address. An email invitation is sent immediately, with the option to resend pending invites if needed.

The Era of Augmented Reality and Making the Most of It

Augmented Reality (AR) is a rapidly advancing technology that overlays digital information onto the real world, creating immersive and interactive experiences. Businesses across industries are leveraging AR to drive innovation.

Here are notable ways AR is shaping the future of business.

  • Streamlined Operations: AR can optimize various business operations, such as training and maintenance. By overlaying digital instructions and guidance onto real-world tasks, AR reduces training time, improves accuracy, and minimizes errors in complex processes.

  • Remote Collaboration: AR facilitates remote collaboration by allowing teams to share information and interact with virtual objects in real time. This technology enables seamless communication, enhances productivity, and breaks down geographical barriers.

  • Marketing and Advertising: AR opens innovative avenues for marketing and advertising campaigns. Interactive AR experiences, such as gamification and virtual tours, capture attention, increase brand awareness, and drive customer engagement.

As the capabilities of AR continue to evolve, businesses that embrace this technology stand to gain a competitive edge. By leveraging AR to create immersive experiences, optimize operations, and unlock new opportunities, businesses can position themselves at the forefront of the augmented reality revolution.
