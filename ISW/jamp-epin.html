Purchase Your JAMB ePin on Quickteller

We're excited to let you know that you can now purchase your JAMB ePin on Quickteller. It's fast, simple and convenient.

How to get your ePin :
Step 1 - SMS 'NIN', space and your 11-Digit National Identification Number (NIN) to 55019 or 66019 e.g (NIN 00123456789)

Step 2 - You'll receive an SMS with your 10-Digit Profile Code and your Registered NIN Name.

Step 3 - Visit www.quickteller.com/jamb2024pinvending to buy ePin using the 10-Digit Profile Code you received via SMS. Your ePin will be delivered to your registered phone number

Step 4 - Register with your ePin at any CBT centre.

UTME registration has commenced and will close on February 26, 2023. You can register for UTME (6,200) or UTME with Mock (7,700).

DE registration commences on Wednesday 28th February and will close on 28th March 2024.
